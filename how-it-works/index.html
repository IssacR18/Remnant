Capture · Remaster · Relive

Your technician leads each phase and keeps you updated with real-time status alerts.

  1. 01

    Capture · In-Person Scanning Session

    We map your space with spatial lidar and volumetric gaussian splats, with the option of layering ambient audio, and remastering light in a single pass.

    • Pre-session communication to preserve important artifacts.
    • Custom hardware with synchronized multi-slam sensors and calibrated AI lidar to record every detail in a single sweep.
    • Multi-array microphones for immersive playback.
    • On-site digital preservation of memory utilizing latest scanning tools.
  2. 02

    Remaster · Neural refinement

    Our pipeline refines 3D Gaussian Splats, develops audio, and rebuilds every surface digitally, while preserving the authenticity of the moment.

    • AI-assisted cleanup with technicians supervising every frame.
    • Post Processing starts with adjusting colission and ends with relighting the scene.
    • Secure encryption before the experience leaves our facility.
  3. 03

    Relive · Vault delivery

    We hand off your memory to your private Memory Vault with web, headset, and computer access. Invite collaborators or family, manage permissions, and most importantly revisit anytime.

    • Guided orientation session for your first walkthrough.
    • Shareable highlights and family-friendly viewing links.
    • Ongoing storage, revisioning, and optional public showcases.
