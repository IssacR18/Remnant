Capture · Remaster · Relive

Your archivist leads each phase and keeps you updated with real-time status alerts.

  1. 01

    Capture · PortalCam session

    We map your space with spatial lidar and volumetric gaussian splats, layering ambient audio, light probes, and annotations in a single pass.

    • Pre-session briefing to stage important artifacts.
    • Multi-array microphones for immersive playback.
    • On-site compression for secure transfer to Remnant Cloud.
  2. 02

    Remaster · Neural refinement

    Our pipeline refines light scattering, denoises audio, and rebuilds every surface with cinematic fidelity—all while preserving the authenticity of the moment.

    • AI-assisted cleanup with human colorists supervising each frame.
    • Semantic tagging so memories are searchable inside your vault.
    • Secure encryption before the experience leaves our facility.
  3. 03

    Relive · Vault delivery

    We hand off a private Remnant Vault with web, headset, and tablet access. Invite collaborators or family, manage permissions, and revisit anytime.

    • Guided orientation session for your first walkthrough.
    • Shareable highlights and time-based bookmarks.
    • Ongoing storage, versioning, and optional public showcases.
