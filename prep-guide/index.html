Capture Session Preparation

Set the stage for a flawless 3D memory.

To make sure your memory is preserved in full detail, it’s best to prepare your space before we arrive. The cleaner and more open the area, the more realistic and beautiful your 3D result will look.

01

Space Preparation

Shape the scene before we arrive

If you want to leave everything exactly as it is, go for it — we’ll scan it as-is. Otherwise a few quick updates make the capture shine:

  • Clear clutter from tables, floors, and countertops.
  • Hide personal or private items you don’t want captured.
  • Turn on all lights to give the LiDAR consistent brightness.
  • Close blinds halfway to minimize harsh sunlight or glare.
  • Keep pets in another room while we’re capturing the space.
02

Day-of-Capture Checklist

Quick reminders before we knock

  • Ready the space

    Make sure the area is ready 10–15 minutes before your session.

  • Flag fragile items

    Tell us about any fragile or valuable items we should avoid touching.

  • Define boundaries

    Review which spaces you do or don’t want included in the capture.

03

For the Client

How to participate in the session

  • You don’t need to be in the room unless you’d like to guide the shot.
  • If you want to appear in the scan, wear neutral clothing without reflective materials and be ready to hold still for up to 3 minutes.
  • You’re welcome to observe — captures usually take 20–60 minutes per space.
  • We’ll confirm on-site once the data looks complete.
04

After the Capture

What happens once we pack up

  • Enhancements

    Optional add-ons include audio hotspots, narration, or walk-through integration.

  • Processing window

    Typical processing takes 2–5 days depending on your plan.

  • Delivery to your vault

    You’ll receive an email once your scan is processed and uploaded to your Remnant Vault.

  • Follow-up options

    Request edits, trims, or even a re-capture if something wasn’t right.

Legal & Safety

A quick privacy reminder

By preparing your space and removing personal items, you agree that any visible items in the scene may appear in the final capture. Remnant respects all privacy and property rights. Please notify your technician if any area is off-limits.
